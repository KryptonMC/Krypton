PlayPacketHandler

class PlayPacketHandler(server: KryptonServer, val connection: NettyConnection, player: KryptonPlayer) : TickablePacketHandler

This is the largest and most important of the four packet handlers, as the play state is where the vast majority of packets reside.

As mentioned above, this is the packet handler for the Play state.

This handles all supported inbound packets in the play state.
