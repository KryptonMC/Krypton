VoxelShape

abstract class VoxelShape(val shape: DiscreteVoxelShape)

The shape of a voxel object. This is used for block shapes, as blocks are all voxels, uniform 1 metre x 1 metre x 1 metre cubes in a locked grid.

This shape also handles other shapes on different faces, as the shape of a block may vary depending on the face it is viewed from. For example, the shape of a cauldron from the bottom or any of the sides is a regular cube, but the face on the top is a regular cube minus a smaller inside cube.
