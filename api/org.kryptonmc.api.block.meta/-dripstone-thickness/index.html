DripstoneThickness

enum DripstoneThickness : Enum<DripstoneThickness>

Indicates the thickness of a dripstone block that forms the multi block stalagmites and stalactites.

As stalagmites and stalactites are spikes on the ceiling or the ground, and they vary in size, it is important to be able to track which part of it a specific block is. For example, a three block stalagmite may have a base, the part connected to the ground, a middle, the section between the base and the top, and the tip, the section at the top that ends the stalagmite.
